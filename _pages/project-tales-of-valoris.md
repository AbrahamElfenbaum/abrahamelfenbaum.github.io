## Overview

Tales of Valoris: Swallow's Defenders is a fantasy tower defense game published by Digital Scorpion Interactive. Players defend Swallow's Keep across 40 campaign levels and an unlockable endless mode, commanding 13 unique defenders — archers, warriors, and mages — against waves of bandits, thieves, and bosses across eight distinct environments.

I worked on this title as both a systems developer and QA Lead.

---

## My Contributions

### Endless Mode
Designed and implemented the systems powering the game's Endless Mode — the infinitely scaling challenge unlocked after completing Act I. This included wave progression logic, difficulty scaling, and leaderboard integration.

### Save / Load System
Built the save and load system used throughout the game, handling persistent player progress, level completion state, and settings.

### Bug Report System
Developed an in-game bug reporting system to allow internal testers and team members to submit reports directly from within the game.

### Achievements
Implemented the Steam achievements system, wiring up all 52 achievements to the appropriate in-game events.

### Bug Fixing
Assisted in resolving bugs reported across development, working across systems as issues were identified and distributed.

### QA Lead
Led quality assurance for the project. Responsible for logging, reproducing, and distributing bug reports to the appropriate teams across the studio. Coordinated testing efforts to ensure issues were tracked and resolved before release.
