## Overview

Frogdoku is a 3D grid-based puzzle game published on Steam by Digital Scorpion Interactive. Players arrange lily pads into viable paths and leap across them as Konna, a clever frog navigating peaceful wetland environments. Drawing inspiration from Sudoku and block puzzles, the game starts simple and builds into intricate brain teasers, all wrapped in a cozy, minimalist art style with ambient soundscapes.
