## Overview

Hit n' Bit is a dark fantasy Metroidvania platformer in development at Digital Scorpion Interactive. Players control two royal siblings — Hitendra, a sword-wielding warrior, and Thybit, a ranged mage — across two distinct realms as they work to prevent an apocalyptic collapse. The game features exploration, boss battles, ability unlocks, and a pixel art style spanning diverse biomes.

I worked on this project as both a developer and QA Lead, contributing to the publicly available Steam demo.
