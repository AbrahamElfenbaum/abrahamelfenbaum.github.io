## Overview

White and Black was my capstone project at Full Sail University, developed with three other programmers from June to September 2022. It is a sandbox god simulator inspired by the classic game *Black and White*. Players become the god of a village with the goal of expanding their divine influence by taking over other villages through resource management, building placement, and spellcasting.

---

## My Contributions

### UI
Implemented the full UI system — menu navigation, HUD layout, and all visual elements the player interacts with throughout the game.

### Building Placement
Created the building placement system, handling player input, resource availability checks, and placement validation based on position and terrain.

### Spell System
Built the complete spell system, including:
- Unlocking spells as the player progresses
- A gesture-based casting mechanic where players input button patterns before casting
- Per-spell effects and activation logic
- Visual feedback for each spell

### Audio
Assigned and implemented all in-game audio — sounds triggered by player actions and ambient sounds for villager work activities.
