## Overview

A procedural terrain generator built as an introductory Unreal Engine assignment at Full Sail University. The goal was to learn Unreal's fundamentals while also exploring how terrain generation works under the hood.

The generator uses Perlin noise and mathematical calculations to produce varied terrain types procedurally. While basic by current standards, this project was an early look into procedural generation concepts that continue to be relevant in game development.
